Why Van Board

The machine is the difference

Most campervan furniture is cut by hand. Ours isn't. Every panel comes off a CNC bed in our own unit, which is why the corners are radiused, the hardware sits flush, and the second kit fits exactly like the first.

It also means we're not tied to a catalogue. If your van isn't one we've done before, we draw it, cut it, and build it.
